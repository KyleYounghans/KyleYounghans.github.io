Wind Speed vs Latitude

Wind speed is not correlated to latitude. Generally speaking, wind speed ranges from 0 to 15mph across all latitudes. Of course, some outliers do exist, but it is not due to the latitude of the locations. As a result, we can conclude that wind speed is not correlated to latitude.
